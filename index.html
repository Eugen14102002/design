Frequently Asked Questions

Below you can see the most common questions we receive with their answers. You can contact us at any time for any questions regarding the website you want.

  • Why choose us?

    Our experience and seriousness in this field have helped us make a name for ourselves in this field today. We create your own website no matter what area you are in. The biggest advantage is that you will speak directly to a design manager who will understand your needs and will not lose information.

  • How will your website be accessible to everyone?

    Another advantage is that we create your website and we make sure it is accessible to everyone. The price you will pay includes a custom domain for your website. We will put your website on the internet and we will connect you the custom domain purchased.

  • Can information be changed for your website after its publication?

    Yeah, definitely. We will change them in case you want to change something. Revisions can be paid whenever you need or a monthly maintenance subscription. It depends a lot on your needs.

  • How do you create all these sites and which platforms you use?

    For most of the sites we built we used pure code, HTML CSS JavaScript ReactJS and we published them on the internet via GitHub. We also use Shopify because it helps us reduce the delivery time for the website and we use it most often for ecommerce sites.

  • What is the best option for buying a website and maintaining it?

    On this question we can help our clients choose a package only after we understand their needs. For example, if you want a website that requires periodic changes, we recommend a monthly subscription depending on how many and what changes you need. If you want a website like portfolio, presentation, etc., you can buy one directly without paying a subscription. As I said above, if you buy a website and after a while you will need a few change you can contact us anytime and we will help you with the best love!
